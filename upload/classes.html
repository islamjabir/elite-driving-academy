Classes

Beginner's Driving Course

The Beginner's Driving Course is designed for those with little to no driving experience. This comprehensive course covers the basics of driving, including vehicle control, road signs, traffic rules, and basic maneuvers. Students will gain confidence behind the wheel through a mix of classroom instruction and hands-on driving practice.

Duration: 6 Weeks Level: Beginner Schedule: 2 sessions per week, 1.5 hours per session Price: $300

Intermediate Driving Course

The Intermediate Driving Course is perfect for those who have some driving experience but need more practice and confidence to drive independently. This course focuses on more advanced driving skills, such as navigating different types of roads, handling various traffic situations, and improving overall driving proficiency.

Duration: 4 Weeks Level: Intermediate Schedule: 2 sessions per week, 2 hours per session Price: $250

Defensive Driving Course

The Defensive Driving Course is aimed at experienced drivers who want to enhance their driving skills and safety awareness. This intensive course teaches advanced defensive driving techniques to help prevent accidents and handle emergencies on the road.

Duration: 2 Weeks Level: Advanced Schedule: 2 sessions per week, 3 hours per session Price: $200
